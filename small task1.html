Debut

Virat Kohli made his international debut for India in 2008 in an ODI match against Sri Lanka. He later made his Test debut in 2011 against West Indies and his T20 debut in 2010.

Career Highlights

  • Former captain of the Indian cricket team.
  • One of the fastest players to score 8000, 9000, 10000 ODI runs.
  • Won ICC Cricketer of the Year awards.
  • Part of the 2011 World Cup winning team.
  • Known for aggressive batting and fitness.
  • Kohli was the captain of the 2008 U19 World Cup winning team.

Marriage Life

Virat Kohli married Bollywood actress Anushka Sharma on December 11, 2017, in a private ceremony in Tuscany, Italy. They first met in 2013 while shooting for a television advertisement. Over time, their friendship turned into a strong relationship. The couple is known for supporting each other's careers and often appears together at public events. In January 2021, they welcomed their daughter named Vamika. Virat and Anushka are considered one of the most popular celebrity couples in India.
